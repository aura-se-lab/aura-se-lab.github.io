On the Effectiveness of LLM-as-a-Judge for Code Generation and Summarization.
G. Crupi, R. Tufano, A. Velasco, A. Mastropaolo, D. Poshyvanyk, G. Bavota.
A controlled evaluation of the LLM-as-a-Judge paradigm across code generation and summarisation. We measure agreement against a curated human gold set; report where it agrees, where it does not, and the calibration gap most prior work omits.
