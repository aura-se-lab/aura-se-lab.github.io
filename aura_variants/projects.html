PROJECT 01 · headline

Trustworthy & Resource-Efficient Foundation Models for SE

active · year 1 of 2
funded by
NSF CRII Award #2451058
$175,000 · 06/2025 → 05/2027 · sole PI
efficiencyreliabilityPEFTdistillation

The lab's headline initiative. We are developing resource-efficient code intelligence that can be trusted under realistic deployment constraints. The project integrates PEFT, quantization, distillation, and rigorous evaluation methodology across three deliverables: a distilled summarisation model (FORGE 2025 ✓), an empirical quantisation atlas (ICSME 2025 ✓), and a joint accuracy-energy benchmark (BRACE, JAWs 2026 ✓).
