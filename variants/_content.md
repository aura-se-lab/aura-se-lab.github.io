## Lab statement (use verbatim or lightly trimmed; do not rewrite)

> The AURA Lab, founded in Fall 2024, advances research at the intersection
> of efficiency, automation, and explainability in software engineering. Our
> mission is to design innovative and responsible AI-driven solutions that
> improve developer productivity while addressing key challenges of
> transparency and resource efficiency. Through rigorous methods, open
> collaboration, and a commitment to impactful work, the AURA Lab contributes
> practical advances in AI-supported software engineering for academia,
> industry, and society.

## Research avenues (four)

1. **Explainability & Interpretability** — methods that reveal how code
   models reason (rationales, traces, tests) so developers can inspect,
   trust, and debug automation.
2. **Efficiency & Resource-Aware Automation** — quantization, distillation,
   and PEFT to deliver low-latency, memory-light, cost-aware automation
   measured on real workloads with reproducible metrics.
3. **Multi-Agent LLMs for SE** — role-aligned agents that plan, code, test,
   and review, coordinated to produce coherent, end-to-end developer
   assistance.
4. **Neurosymbolic AI for SE** — hybrid pipelines pairing LLMs with program
   analysis and constraints to produce interpretable, checkable fixes,
   tests, and designs.
5. **Task-Aware Code Automation** — from issues, diffs, and traces to
   concise summaries, TODOs, and plans; models that reduce developer toil
   with actionable, repo-aware guidance.

(Yes, five — the site lists four primary projects and one supporting
