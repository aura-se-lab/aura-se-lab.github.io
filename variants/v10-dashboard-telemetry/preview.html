The AI for Understandable and Responsible Automation Lab

The AURA Lab advances research at the intersection of efficiency, automation, and explainability in software engineering. Our mission is to design innovative and responsible AI-driven solutions that improve developer productivity while addressing key challenges of transparency and resource efficiency. Through rigorous methods, open collaboration, and a commitment to impactful work, the lab contributes practical advances in AI-supported software engineering for academia, industry, and society.
